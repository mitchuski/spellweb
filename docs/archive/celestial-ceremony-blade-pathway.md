## Running the Ceremony

### Solo (mapping your own understanding)

Forge both blades yourself. The Sun blade first — trace the disclosure path, forge. Then the Moon blade — trace the reflection path, forge. Hold both. The pair is your reading of the architecture.

### Bilateral (the Celestial Ceremony)

**Person A** forges the Sun blade. Shares it. **Gap.** Both enter the Moon ceremony together (three songs + Amnesia Protocol). Then:

**Reflect (night):** Person B modifies the Amnesia Path constellation based on what the Moon ceremony revealed — adds nodes, shifts edges — and forges their own Moon blade on altered ground. Two blades. Blade pair.

**Connect (day):** Person B witnesses both constellations. Carries the understanding forward. No counter-forge.

### The Progression

You don't need to forge blades to run this ceremony. The three depths apply:

🔑 **Understanding** — listen to the songs and poems together. Two phones. The experience is the key.

✦ **Constellation** — open spellweb.ai, trace these paths (or your own). The map is the second depth.

🗡️ **Blade** — hit evoke. The proof is the third depth. The blade is the boundary made permanent.

Each level is complete on its own.
