## Reading the Phase

**🌑 New Moon (Hex 00)** — nothing is active. No boundaries forged. The information space is total and undisclosed. This is not a blade — it is the absence of a blade. The null vertex on the lattice.

**🌒 Waxing Crescent (Stratum 1)** — one dimension active. A sliver of the Swordsman's boundary is visible. The smallest possible proof. One edge. One commitment. The rest is dark.

**🌓 First Quarter (Stratum 2)** — two dimensions active. The dual-agent blade (Protection + Delegation, 1,1,0,0,0,0) lives at this phase. Half dark, half lit. The Swordsman and the Mage as a single crescent — visible enough to prove the separation exists, dark enough to hide everything else.

**🌔 Waxing Gibbous (Stratum 3)** — three dimensions active. The midpoint. Three axes of the six-dimensional sovereignty space are reflected. The blade is becoming substantial.

**🌖 Waning Gibbous (Stratum 4)** — four dimensions active. More light than dark. The proof is generous — four boundaries visible, two held private.

**🌗 Last Quarter (Stratum 5)** — five dimensions active. Near-full. One dimension remains dormant — one edge of the sovereignty space held dark. The blade at Hex 3E (111110) carries this phase: everything burning except Value. The crescent of darkness is the last privacy.

**🌕 Full Moon (Hex 3F)** — all six dimensions active. Full sovereignty reflected. The Universe Blade. 乾, The Creative. Nothing held dark. The Swordsman's boundary encompasses the entire space and reflects all of it. This is not full disclosure of content — the ZK property still holds. The constellation path, the poems, the discussion remain private. But the sovereignty POSTURE is fully visible: all six dimensions are active, all boundaries are forged.

## The ZK Distinction

The moon phase shows the **sovereignty posture** — which dimensions are active. It does NOT show the **content** — what was actually discussed, traced, or forged within those dimensions. 

A 🌕 Full Moon blade proves "I have all six dimensions active" without revealing which specific nodes activated them, which poems were spoken, or which conversation happened in the gap. The phase is the WHAT. The content is the HOW. ZK protects the HOW.

The Swordsman's boundary determines the phase. The zero-knowledge property protects what happens within the boundary.
