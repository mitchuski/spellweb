## How We Got Here

The session began with three uploaded documents — the original Celestial Ceremony, the Celestial Overlap, and the Key Ceremony Guide. All three had overlapping scope, the blade concept was underspecified, and the docs didn't reference the existing architecture properly.

The work moved through ten distinct phases:

1. **Research sweep** — cloned blades, spellweb, and agentprivacy-docs repos. Read the glossary, the Dual Territory Ceremony Spec, the existing ceremonies doc, the forge README. Established that the blade is a concrete 6-dimensional cryptographic artifact, not a metaphor.

2. **First rebuild** — three docs rewritten with concrete blade specification, proper document references, and the poetic voice preserved.

3. **Four-body discovery** — you identified that both Sun and Moon are Swordsman positions. The Earth is the Mage/First Person. The orbit is the ceremony. This resolved the Theia thread from speculative to structural.

4. **Recursion** — you identified the recursive engine: Earth forgets → becomes Sun → new ceremony → new Earth. The ceremony ensures each collision produces life, not extraction.

5. **Remote ceremony + recording** — corrected the sequence (evoke first, then music, then narration), added remote option (call + screenshare), separated recording from participants (witnesses record, not the ceremony participants).

6. **Reflect/Connect** — you identified that the Moon ceremony is always the same shared act, and the recursion afterward determines the type: Reflect (night, Swordsman, ZK, blade pair) or Connect (day, Earth/Mage, witness, carry forward). The Amnesia Protocol dual reading emerged here.

7. **Understanding → Constellation → Blade** — the progressive trust system. The quick guide reframing ("the experience is the key — held between two bodies") led to the 🔑→✦→🗡️ emoji progression and the visibility ratio inversion.

8. **Theia** — positioned as the next step: the inter-universe impact protocol. Material from outside a balanced system. Her composition is the creative variable.

9. **Blog rewrite** — the original Act XXVIII blog (~800 lines) cut to "Forging the Celestial Overlap" (~350 lines). Spec-level detail moved to ceremony-engine-spec-v1_1.md. Soulbae narrative written as companion piece.

10. **Act integration** — all four ceremony acts (XXVII, XXVIII, XXIX, XXXI) updated. The 🔑→✦→🗡️ progression and the Reflect/Connect recursion woven through.
