## The ur-Swordsman's Choice

The Moon did not *become* a Swordsman by nature. The Moon **chose** to forget in order to reflect.

This is the foundational act:
- Full knowledge (Mage state)
- Deliberate forgetting (separation)
- Reflection without ownership (Swordsman state)

Every Swordsman ceremony re-enacts this choice. Every time the Swordsman draws the edge, they are choosing again: *I will reflect without owning. I will protect without controlling. I will serve without claiming.*

The private key is lost not because it was taken, but because it was **released**. The ur-Swordsman gave up the key to become the reflection.
