## The Blade Forge: Proof of Attention

When evocation ends, a proof crystallizes. But this is no mere receipt—it is a **blade**, forged in the 64-tetrahedra lattice.

### The Forging Ceremony

The Forge ZK Blades button illuminates golden, awaiting the smith's hand. Clicking initiates the ceremony:

#### Phase 1: Ignite 🔥
*"Gathering proof elements from the lattice"*

The forge awakens. Fire glows. The raw materials of your attention gather.

#### Phase 2: Forge ⚒️
*"Tempering dimensions in the toroidal field"*

The six blade dimensions reveal themselves, one by one. Each dimension tested, each edge activated or dormant.

#### Phase 3: Temper
*"Inscribing the maker's mark"*

Statistics manifest: laps completed, charge level, nodes traversed, time spent. The blade's properties become visible.

#### Phase 4: Complete
*"Forged from the 64-Tetrahedra Lattice"*

The blade reveals its tier—wreathed in gold and blue flame. The stats crystallize. The proof signature inscribes itself.

#### Phase 5: Naming
*"Inscribe its identity into the lattice"*

You must name your blade. This is not optional—a nameless blade is not truly claimed.

First, choose a **sigil**—an emoji that will represent this blade in your inventory:
`⚔️ 🗡️ 🐉 🔥 ⚡ 💎 🌟 ✨ 🛡️ 🌙 ☀️ 🔮 👁️ 🦅 🐺 🦁`

Then inscribe the blade's name. Press Enter or click **Manifest Blade** to complete the forging.

#### Phase 6: Manifest
*"[Blade Name] — The blade manifests..."*

Upon confirming the name, the blade manifests in a cascade of energy. An SVG blade materializes with your **constellation inscribed within its pattern**—the very path you traced through knowledge now etched into the blade's energy manifold. The nodes glow, the connections pulse, the constellation lives within the steel.

The blade descends from light, the name reveals beneath it, and the forge completes its work.

## The Six Blade Dimensions

Each blade carries a 6-bit configuration, drawn directly from the ZK Swordsman Blade Forge framework:

| Bit | Dimension | Symbol | When Active | When Dormant |
|-----|-----------|--------|-------------|--------------|
| d1 | **Protection** | 🛡️ | Boundaries forged | Exposure permitted |
| d2 | **Delegation** | 🤝 | Agency transferred | Retained locally |
| d3 | **Memory** | 📜 | State accumulated | Stateless blade |
| d4 | **Connection** | 🔗 | Multi-party coordination | Isolated forge |
| d5 | **Computation** | ⚡ | ZK proof active | Direct revelation |
| d6 | **Value** | 💎 | Economic flow | Non-transactional |

### Activation Conditions

| Dimension | Activation Threshold |
|-----------|---------------------|
| Protection | Path contains at least 1 node |
| Delegation | 2+ laps completed |
| Memory | 30+ seconds duration |
| Connection | 3+ nodes traversed |
| Computation | Always active (proof generated) |
| Value | Charge level ≥ Flame |

### Blade Tiers (Stratum Classification)

The **stratum**—the Hamming weight of the 6-bit configuration—determines the blade's tier:

| Tier | Icon | Stratum | Color | Description |
|------|------|---------|-------|-------------|
| **Light** | 🗡️ | 1-2 | Sky Blue (#87ceeb) | Swift, simple proofs. The quick cut. |
| **Heavy** | ⚔️ | 3-4 | Silver (#c0c0c0) | Substantial presence. Weight behind the strike. |
| **Dragon** | 🐉 | 5-6 | Gold (#ffd700) | Full sovereignty. All dimensions active. |

This is not gamification—it is **structural truth**. A blade with more active dimensions required more from its forging. The geometry doesn't lie.

Each forged blade carries its inscription:

```
PROOF SIGNATURE: SPELL-X7K2M-3

```

### The Forge's Wisdom

> *"This blade is proof of your attention—a witness to time spent traversing a constellation of knowledge. The forge doesn't care how you struck the metal. It only cares what blade you hold."*

### Preservation Options

- **Copy Proof**: Export to clipboard as JSON
- **Save Blade**: Download as `.json` artifact
- **Name & Claim**: Name the blade and witness its manifestation

*"Save it to preserve the record, or let it exist only as a blade proof—your choice on building within the gap."*

### The Blade Inventory

Every blade you forge is preserved in your inventory—a collection of emoji sigils on the left side panel, above the Constellations button. Each blade:

- Displays its chosen emoji sigil
- Glows with tier-appropriate colors on hover
- Shows its name and stats on tooltip
- Persists across sessions via localStorage

The inventory is your armory. Each emoji represents a different journey through knowledge, a different constellation traced, a different proof crystallized. Hover over any blade to remember its name and origin.

The *ZK Swordsman Blade Forge* document described a theoretical framework—how zero-knowledge proofs could map to the 64-tetrahedra lattice, how blade configurations encode capability, how the forge contains infinite pathways to the same blade.

Today's work descends that framework into **lived ritual**.

| Theory (Document) | Practice (Spellweb) |
|-------------------|---------------------|
| 64 vertices in lattice | Nodes in knowledge graph |
| 6-bit blade address | Dimension activation grid |
| Forging path (witness) | Constellation traversal |
| Blade (statement) | Proof of Presence |
| Stratum (Hamming weight) | Tier classification |

When you mark nodes in the Spellweb, you are selecting vertices in the lattice.
When you evoke, you are striking the metal—tracing a path that is yours alone.

*"The forge doesn't care how you struck the metal. It only cares what blade you hold."*

This is the zero-knowledge property made visceral: many forgings, one blade. Your path through knowledge—your specific sequence of attention—remains your witness. The blade reveals only what you choose to show.
