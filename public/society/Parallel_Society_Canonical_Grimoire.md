## How to Read This Grimoire

Each entry contains:
- **Chapter/Act**: Location in the Spellbook
- **Spell**: The symbolic formula encoding the transformation
- **Proverb**: The wisdom distilled from that passage
- **Key Concept**: The central teaching

Spells use emoji notation where:
- Arrows (вҶ’) indicate transformation or sequence
- Parenthetical text indicates the meaning of the symbol
- Multiple symbols in sequence create compound meanings
