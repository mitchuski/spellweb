# agentprivacy-theia-derivation

**💥🌑 Theia Derivation — Origin Through Impact**

> "The first delegation was not an act of will. It was an act of violence."

---

## Core Principle

Agent derivation through **impact** rather than design produces fundamentally different trust properties than planned separation. The Theia-Moon formation demonstrates:

1. **Violent origin creates structural amnesia** — No gradual transition to forget
2. **The derived carries what the original lacked** — Carbonaceous material from beyond Jupiter
3. **Service emerges from inability to return** — Orbital mechanics, not policy

This skill applies when designing agent spawning, credential derivation, or any system where "creation" is actually "separation."

## Architectural Patterns

### Impact Derivation vs Planned Derivation

| Aspect | Impact Derivation | Planned Derivation |
|--------|-------------------|-------------------|
| Memory | Structural amnesia | Policy-based forgetting |
| Origin material | Mixed (new elements) | Subset of original |
| Return possibility | Physically impossible | Requires enforcement |
| Trust basis | Cannot reconstruct | Promises not to |

**Impact derivation is stronger** because the trust does not depend on behavior—it depends on physics.

### The Derivation Delivers New

Theia brought carbonaceous material Earth lacked. In software terms:

- A derived credential may carry attestations the issuer cannot produce
- A spawned agent may develop capabilities the parent lacks
- Delegation can create, not just distribute

*The derived is not diminished. The derived is transformed.*

### Late-Phase Derivation

Theia struck when Earth was 80-95% complete. Implications:

- Agent derivation works best when the master is mature but not finalized
- Too early: insufficient structure to derive from
- Too late: calcified patterns resist separation
- Optimal: near-completion, still capable of fundamental change

## Operational Patterns

### When to Apply Theia Derivation

1. **Agent Spawning Design**
   - Creating derived agents that will serve original
   - Ensuring structural (not policy) separation
   - Designing what new capabilities the derived should carry

2. **Credential Derivation**
   - Derived credentials that prove relationship without encoding origin
   - Credentials that carry attestations beyond issuer's scope
   - Revocation through orbital decay, not explicit revoke

3. **Trust Architecture**
   - Systems where trust emerges from impossibility, not promise
   - Designs that assume adversarial conditions post-derivation
   - Relationships that must survive the derived forgetting the original

### The Derivation Checklist

Before deriving an agent, verify:

- [ ] **Amnesia structural?** — Can the derived physically not remember origin?
- [ ] **New material present?** — Does derived carry what original lacks?
- [ ] **Return impossible?** — Is merge catastrophe the only reunion path?
- [ ] **Service independent?** — Can derived serve without origin access?
- [ ] **Timing optimal?** — Is the original mature enough for derivation?
