## The Canonical Sources by Era

The archive organized itself into eras:

**Era I: Foundational Runes (1983-1997)**

David Chaum: "Blind Signatures for Untraceable Payments" (1983)—first blind signature scheme, foundation of privacy-preserving payments. "Security Without Identification" (1985)—pseudonymous credentials, "Big Brother obsolete" thesis.

Timothy May: "The Crypto Anarchist Manifesto" (1988)—political framing of cryptography, predictions of crypto-enabled coordination.

Eric Hughes: "A Cypherpunk's Manifesto" (1993)—"Privacy is the power to selectively reveal oneself," "Cypherpunks write code."

Proof of Work: Dwork and Naor "Pricing via Processing" (1992)—original proof-of-work concept. Adam Back "Hashcash" (1997)—practical implementation, foundation for Bitcoin mining.

**Era II: Early Incantations (1997-2007)**

Nick Szabo: "The Idea of Smart Contracts" (1997)—vending machine analogy, code as self-enforcing agreement. "Bit Gold" (published 2008)—chained proof-of-work, proto-Bitcoin architecture.

Wei Dai: "b-money" (1998)—peer-maintained ledgers, decentralized currency concept.

Davidson and Rees-Mogg: "The Sovereign Individual" (1997)—political prophecy, transition period warnings.

**Era III: The Synthesis (2008-2014)**

Satoshi Nakamoto: "Bitcoin: A Peer-to-Peer Electronic Cash System" (October 31, 2008)—the synthesis whitepaper. Genesis Block (January 3, 2009)—"Chancellor on brink of second bailout for banks." Forum posts and emails (2008-2010)—design decisions, privacy acknowledgments.

Canonical Events: Pizza Day (May 22, 2010)—first commercial transaction. Mt. Gox collapse (February 2014)—"not your keys, not your coins." Satoshi's disappearance (December 2010)—reference loss as cryptographic act.

**Era IV: The World Computer (2014-2016)**

Vitalik Buterin: Ethereum whitepaper (2013)—generalized blockchain vision. Various blog posts explaining design decisions.

Gavin Wood: Yellow Paper (2014)—formal specification of Ethereum Virtual Machine.

The DAO: Launch (April 2016), exploit (June 17, 2016), fork decision (July 20, 2016), Ethereum Classic emergence.

**Era V: The Schism (2016-2022)**

Financial Canon sources: Uniswap whitepaper (2018), Compound whitepaper (2019), MakerDAO documentation, DeFi Summer analysis.

Social Canon sources: Weyl, Buterin, Hitzig "Liberal Radicalism" (2018), Srinivasan "The Network State" (2022), Buterin "My Techno-Optimism" (2023).

Joel Monegro: "Fat Protocols" (2016)—shaped investment theses.

**Era VI: Surveillance Truth (2020-2025)**

Chain Analysis: Chainalysis government contracts (public records), industry valuations, capability reports.

Tornado Cash: OFAC designation (August 8, 2022), court filings, Fifth Circuit ruling (November 2024).

Pertsev case: Arrest (August 10, 2022), trial proceedings, verdict (May 2024)—"ideology of maximum privacy" as evidence.

**Era VII: The Missing Primitive (2024-2025)**

Privacy Pools: Buterin, Soleimani et al. paper (2023)—compliant privacy proposal.

Production Privacy: Aztec documentation, Namada specifications, Zcash developments.

Regulatory Evolution: GENIUS Act, stablecoin legislation, jurisdictional variations.
