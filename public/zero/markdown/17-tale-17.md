**The Ceremony:**

Ceremonius showed them the **Powers of Tau ceremony**:

"We need to generate τ and compute g^τ^i for i = 0 to N, then **destroy τ forever**."

He demonstrated the **Multi-Party Computation (MPC)** protocol:

```
Round 1: Alice generates τ₁, computes g^(τ₁^i), destroys τ₁
```

"The beautiful property: **If even ONE participant is honest**, the ceremony is secure!"

He explained why:

"Suppose 99 participants are malicious and collude. But participant #50 was honest and truly destroyed their τ₅₀. Then no one knows the final τ because it includes τ₅₀ as a factor."
